## Quick Verdict

### Best for Evelina's Concerns:

1. **Pickalbatros Royal Grand** - Best for avoiding kids noise (adults only), clean, but has nightly entertainment
2. **Verginia Sharm** - In "quiet part" of town, has adults-only quiet pool, but older facilities

### Things to Request at Check-in:
- [ ] Room away from pool animation/entertainment area
- [ ] Room away from nightclub/amphitheater
- [ ] Higher floor room (less foot traffic noise)
- [ ] Non-smoking room confirmed

### Red Flags NOT Found:
- No bed bug reports in any 2025-2026 reviews
- No major smoking complaints
- Noise complaints are about entertainment, not construction/traffic
