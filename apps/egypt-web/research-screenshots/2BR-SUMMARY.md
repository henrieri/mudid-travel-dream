- "Family rooms" often have bunk beds in the SAME room - NOT separate
- "Suites" usually mean living room + bedroom - NOT 2 bedrooms
- Even expensive options (€15k villas) sometimes only have 1 bedroom

### Recommendation Priority

2. **Baron Palms - Executive Suite 2BR** (€5,004) - Premium option with 2 bathrooms
